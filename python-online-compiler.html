Python online compiler vs local installation

Should you Stick to the cloud or install locally? Here is a quick comparison:

  • Setup Time: Online Compiler (0 minutes) vs Local (15-30 minutes).
  • Performance: Online is limited by internet speed; Local uses your CPU power (better for heavy tasks).
  • Accessibility: Online works on any device with a browser; Local works only on the installed machine.
  • Libraries: Online compilers often support standard libraries but may limit external ones (like PyGame); Local offers full freedom.
  • Working Offline: Online requires internet; Local works without connection.
